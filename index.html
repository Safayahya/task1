Palestine

Palestine is distinguished by its antiquity, history and distinctive hospitality customs. It is located in the heart of the world and is considered a meeting point between Africa, Europe and Asia. Palestine is characterized by the multiplicity of languages ​​​​used in it and the multiplicity of cultures and beliefs. Palestine is considered the cradle of the heavenly religions and the center of the most important religious landmarks and historical evidence.
